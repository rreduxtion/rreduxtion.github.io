El Rancho Institute

 
 

El Rancho Institute is a computing think tank located at the base of Black Mesa north of Santa Fe, south of Espa√±ola, and east of Los Alamos in Northern New Mexico, USA. The institute offers short-term research and development engagements to work with Dr. Rodriguez and other associates on advancing the following areas of thought:

  • Virtual machines: develop data languages, bytecode specifications, and respective executing machines.
  • Data algebra: develop algebras capable of unifying the numerous data processing languages in existence today.
  • Computational physics: develop theoretical models of fundamental physics based on graph and stream mathematics.

Interested readers desiring an associate position should write an email describing their background, interests, and ultimate goals with El Rancho Institute.

 
 

El Rancho Institute associates enjoy positions lasting up to one month and are provided free private boarding in a small recently remodeled 100 year old fully furnished adobe home. During the warm seasons, El Rancho Institute is a working farm and associates can enjoy spending their leisure time working the land, hiking/motorsports throughout the Pojoaque Valley, or visiting the many tourist locations in Northern New Mexico. During the cold seasons, winter sports abound as numerous ski and snowboard resorts exist through the Sange de Cristo Mountains including the Santa Fe Ski and Taos Ski Valley.

ReduX (pronounced "redux") was founded by Dr. Marko A. Rodriguez in early 2019 with a mission to advance open source graph and stream computing technologies while providing associated consulting services for these technologies.

 

Graph Computing

A graph is a structure composed of vertices and edges. Vertices typically represent objects and edges denote relationships between them. A graph is manipulated and analyzed by graph traversals. A graph traversal is an algorithmic walk through the graph. Graphs have increasingly become a common modeling substrate for complex interconnected domains.

 

Stream Computing

A stream is an ever expanding and contracting list of objects. Streams are joined together by functions which map objects from an incoming stream to objects in an outgoing stream. Streams can be conveniently distributed and parallized and this has enabled them to become the foundation of many modern distributed computing systems.
